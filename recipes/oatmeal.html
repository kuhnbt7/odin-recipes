Oatmeal

Description

To make oatmeal, you add two parts water to one part rolled oats. Add a pinch of salt and season with cinnamon as desired.

Ingredients

  • Rolled oats
  • Salt
  • Cinnamon (optional)

Steps

  1. Add rolled oats to microwave-safe bowl
  2. Add water
  3. Microwave for five minutes
  4. Remove from microwave, stir, and let cool
