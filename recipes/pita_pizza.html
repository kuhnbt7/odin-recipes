Pita pizza

Description

Pita pizzas are deliious and much healthier that the store bought variety

Ingredients

  • Pita bread
  • Pizza sauce
  • Cheese
  • Vegetables (mushrooms, peppers, broccoli, etc)
  • Meat (chicken, pepperoni, sausage, etc)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425
  2. Split pita bread in half and line up on a baking sheet
  3. Add tomato sauce to each pita bread half
  4. Add cheese
  5. Add vegetables evenly spaced
  6. Add meat
  7. Bake in oven for approximately 12 minutes. Remove and let cool before eating
