Quinoa

Description

Oatmeal is a high-protein grain from South American. It is delicious and a good source of whole grains and fiber.

Ingredients

  • Quinoa
  • Water

Steps

  1. Measure out quinoa (1/2 cup is a good amount) and rinse with water to remove bitter taste. Add to a saucepan
  2. Meaure two parts water to one part quinoa and add to saucepan
  3. Bring to a boil on the stove. When water boils, turn down heat to medium
  4. Cook until water is evaporated, then add lid to the saucepan and turn off heat
  5. Let sit for a minute or two, then remove and serve
